CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin

The son of Don Rafael Ibarra who came back to the Philippines after being in Europe for 7 years of studying. He is admired by several people as he is versed in many things.

María Clara

She is the daughter of Padre Damaso, but was raised by Capitan Tiago. When she grew up with Crisóstomo Ibarra as her childhood friend, they planned to marry each other.

Padre Damaso

He is the biological father of María Clara and a friar. He cared deeply for his daughter, but was rude to others.

Elias

When he was in a dangerous situation, Ibarra was the one to help him. Elias felt grateful for Ibarra. In return, he helped him in many incidents.

Capitan Tiago

Unlike Padre Damaso, he was supportive of Ibarra and María's plan to marry each other. He is also wealthy and respected by many.

Padre Sibyla

Padre Sibyla is a young priest. He is often calm and smart.
